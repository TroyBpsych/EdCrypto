Section 02 — Token Architecture

How the Token Works

Two mechanisms, one trigger. When schools hit verified success milestones, smart contracts automatically burn tokens from the treasury and accelerate staff vesting. Every student success makes each remaining token more scarce — and more valuable.

Mechanism 1

Token Burn

When a school hits its milestone targets, a percentage of treasury tokens are permanently destroyed. Less supply with equal or growing demand means higher value per token. Investors are directly rewarded when students succeed.

Mechanism 2

Vesting Acceleration

Staff tokens vest faster when their school hits targets. Hitting all milestones can compress a 4-year vest into 2.5 years. Missing targets doesn't forfeit tokens — it only slows the unlock. The token is compensation, never a threat.

Section 03 — Verification

What Triggers the Token

Six categories of success metrics feed the system. Academic metrics use established, nationally normed assessments — data that can't be manipulated by the schools themselves. All metrics measure growth against each student's own baseline, not absolute scores.

The growth principle: A student moving from the 20th to the 40th percentile triggers the same reward as one moving from 70th to 90th. This ensures schools serving disadvantaged students are never penalized for starting behind. The system rewards improvement, not privilege.

Section 04 — Fairness by Design

The Equity Layer

A student at the 80th percentile has almost no room to grow statistically. A flat growth requirement would penalize schools serving higher-performing students — and disincentivize teachers from working at harder schools. Three equity layers solve this.

Layer 1

Graduated Growth Targets

Growth thresholds shrink as starting percentile rises. Below 40th percentile needs +15 points to trigger. Above 70th only needs +5 points. Same reward, calibrated bar.

Layer 2

Burn Multipliers by Tier

Higher-need schools get a larger burn when they hit milestones. Tier 1 fires at 1.5x. Tier 2 at 1.2x. Tier 3 at 1.0x. Investors backing harder schools earn more when those schools succeed.

Layer 3

Teacher Vesting Differential

Teachers at higher-need schools vest faster from day one — before any milestones. Tier 1: 2.5yr base. Tier 2: 3yr base. Tier 3: 4yr base. Choosing harder schools is financially rational, not a sacrifice.

Section 07 — Compliance

Legal & Regulatory Path

The token almost certainly meets the SEC's Howey Test — it's an investment with expectation of profit from others' efforts. That's not a dealbreaker. It means operating within securities law rather than ignoring it — which is what killed most crypto projects between 2018 and 2024.

The Howey Test: An investment is a security if it involves (1) investment of money, (2) in a common enterprise, (3) with expectation of profits, (4) primarily from the efforts of others. This token checks all four boxes. The answer is compliance, not avoidance.

Phase 1 · Year 0

Reg D 506(c) + Reg S

Reg D allows unlimited raises from accredited US investors with general solicitation — you can advertise publicly as long as you verify investor accreditation. These six rules must be written into the founding charter — not policy documents, not board resolutions. Amendment requires ⅔ supermajority board vote plus 60-day public notice. They are structurally harder than the people who run the organization.

Rule 1 — The 60/25/15 split is constitutional

60% of all Bitcoin holdings are permanent core (Tier A) — they can never be sold, lent, collateralized, or moved for any reason. 25% is strategic reserve (Tier B) — board-approved uses only per the defined trigger table. 15% is emergency reserve (Tier C) — requires ⅔ board vote and 30-day public notice.

Rule 2 — DCA purchase schedule is automatic

Bitcoin purchases from each raise deploy automatically via smart contract over 24 months in equal monthly installments. No single lump purchase. No timing discretion. No board override of the purchase schedule once a raise closes.

Rule 3 — Bonus/penalty is oracle-verified, not board-decided

The ±3% milestone adjustment is determined solely by on-chain oracle verification of the six academic and community metrics. No board vote. No human discretion. The milestone hit or it didn't — the smart contract adjusts the allocation automatically.

Rule 4 — All purchases publicly announced within 72 hours

Every Bitcoin purchase — base or bonus — is announced publicly via the reserve dashboard, press release, and on-chain transaction record within 72 hours of execution. No exceptions. The transparency is the product.

Rule 5 — Strategic reserve sales fund only buyback + burn

The only permitted reason to convert Bitcoin from the strategic reserve into another asset is to fund open-market token buybacks that are immediately burned. Proceeds of any strategic BTC conversion cannot be used for any operational purpose under any circumstances.

Rule 6 — Quarterly NAV report is mandatory

The organization publishes a full Net Asset Value report every quarter — Bitcoin holdings at market price, real estate appraised values, token treasury balance, and total organizational NAV. Any board member may be removed for cause if this report is delayed more than 30 days past quarter close.
